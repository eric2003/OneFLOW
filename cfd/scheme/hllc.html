HLLC Riemann Solver

HLLC Riemann Solver used lower and upper bounds on the characteristics speeds in the solution of Riemann problem involving left and right states. These bounds are approximated as

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{c} S_{L}=\text{min}(u_{L},u_{R})-\text{max}(a_{L},a_{R})\\ S_{R}=\text{max}(u_{L},u_{R})+\text{max}(a_{L},a_{R})\\ \end{array}\end{split}\]

where \(S_{L}\) and \(S_{R}\) are the lower and upper bound for the left and right state characteristics speed. For HLLC scheme we also include middle wave of speed \(S_{*}\) given by

\[S_{*}=\cfrac{p_{R}-p_{L}+\rho_{L}u_{L}(S_{L}-u_{L})-\rho_{R}u_{R}(S_{R}-u_{R})} {\rho_{L}(S_{L}-u_{L})-\rho_{R}(S_{R}-u_{R})}\]

The mean pressure is given by

\[P_{LR}=\cfrac{1}{2}(p_{L}+p_{R}+\rho_{L}(S_{L}-u_{L})(S_{*}-u_{L})+\rho_{R}(S_{R}-u_{R})(S_{*}-u_{R}))\]

The fluxes are computed as

\[\begin{split}F_{i+1/2}=\left\{ \begin{array}{ll} F^{L},& \text{if } S_{L}\ge 0\\ F_{*L},& \text{if }S_{L}\le 0\le S_{*}\\ F_{*R},& \text{if }S_{*}\le 0\le S_{R}\\ F^{R},& \text{if }S_{R}\le 0\\ \end{array} \right.\end{split}\]
\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{l} \mathbf{U}_{*L}=\cfrac{S_{L}\mathbf{U}_{L}-\mathbf{F}_{L}+P_{LR}\mathbf{D}_{*}}{S_{L}-S_{*}}\\ \mathbf{U}_{*R}=\cfrac{S_{R}\mathbf{U}_{R}-\mathbf{F}_{R}+P_{LR}\mathbf{D}_{*}}{S_{R}-S_{*}}\\ \mathbf{F}_{*L}=\cfrac{S_{*}(S_{L}\mathbf{U}_{L}-\mathbf{F}_{L})+S_{L}P_{LR}\mathbf{D}_{*}}{S_{L}-S_{*}}\\ \mathbf{F}_{*R}=\cfrac{S_{*}(S_{R}\mathbf{U}_{R}-\mathbf{F}_{R})+S_{R}P_{LR}\mathbf{D}_{*}}{S_{R}-S_{*}}\\ \end{array}\end{split}\]
\[\mathbf{U}_{*K}=\cfrac{S_{K}\mathbf{U}_{K}-\mathbf{F}_{K}+P_{LR}\mathbf{D}_{*}}{S_{K}-S_{*}}\]
\[\begin{split}\mathbf{F}_{*K}=\cfrac{S_{*}(S_{K}\mathbf{U}_{K}-\mathbf{F}_{K})+S_{K}P_{LR}\mathbf{D}_{*}}{S_{K}-S_{*}}\\\end{split}\]
\[\begin{split}\mathbf{U}=\begin{bmatrix} \rho\\\rho u\\\rho E \end{bmatrix}\quad \mathbf{F}=\begin{bmatrix} \rho u\\\rho u^2+p\\\rho H u \end{bmatrix}\quad \mathbf{D}_{*}=\begin{bmatrix} 0\\1\\S_{*} \end{bmatrix}\end{split}\]
\[\begin{split}\mathbf{U}_{L}=\left[\begin{array}{l} \rho_{L}\\\rho_{L} u_{L}\\\rho_{L} E_{L} \end{array}\right]\quad \mathbf{U}_{R}=\left[\begin{array}{l} \rho_{R}\\\rho_{R} u_{R}\\\rho_{R} E_{R} \end{array}\right]\end{split}\]
\[\begin{split}\mathbf{F}_{L}=\left[\begin{array}{l} \rho_{L}u_{L}\\\rho_{L} u^{2}_{L}+p_{L}\\\rho_{L} H_{L}u_{L} \end{array}\right]\quad \mathbf{F}_{R}=\left[\begin{array}{l} \rho_{R}u_{R}\\\rho_{R} u^{2}_{R}+p_{R}\\\rho_{R} H_{R}u_{R} \end{array}\right]\end{split}\]
